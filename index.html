Hi, I’m Larissa Costa

A researcher activist at University of Texas at Austin

I am an activist, nanny, and migrant focused on community engagement for policy change. My current research focuses on the "Visitor Exchange Program" Au Pair of the United States Department and how political opinions on inequalities and reproductive justice are fostered throughout the migration experience in the care labor industry.
